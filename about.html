About Hotel Lavender

In 2007 the hotel had a £5.5 million refurbishment, making it the highest rated 2010 AA hotel Guide four star hotel in the South of England including London with a quality rating score of 86%.

The hotel is now owned by Smith family and managed by Interstate Hotels. The hotel underwent a £2.8 million refurbishment and reopened as Lavender Hotel – London in December 2016.
